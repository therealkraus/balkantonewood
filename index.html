About us

BalkanToneWood is Canadian based company. We specialise in import of high grade tone wood from Balkan, which has some of the best quality tone wood in Europe.

Maple (Acer Pseudoplatanus) and spruce (Picea Excelsa) from this region has always been prized as highest quality tone wood available. The great violin-makers, including Stradivarius, obtained wood from this region for their instruments.

BalkanToneWood imports high quality product into Canada and focus on distributing to Canadian and USA market. Our goal is to reach luthiers and merchants in Canada and USA and offer them high quality merchandise at affordable price. Our spruce is praised by every luthier as best wood they can find.
